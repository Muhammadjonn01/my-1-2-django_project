PORTFOLIO

A portfolio is a collection of work samples that showcases the skills, talents, and experiences of an individual. It is commonly used in various fields such as art, design, writing, photography, and marketing to demonstrate the quality and range of someone's work.

Resume or CV: A summary of the individual's education, work history, skills, and achievements.

Samples of Work: Examples of the individual's best projects, such as artwork, design mock-ups, writing samples, or marketing campaigns.

Project Descriptions: Descriptions or explanations of the context, objectives, and challenges of each project.

Testimonials or Recommendations: Feedback or endorsements from clients, colleagues, or supervisors.

Skills or Expertise: An overview of the person's technical skills, software proficiency, or areas of expertise.

Personal Statement: A brief introduction or statement about the individual's goals, aspirations, or creative process.
